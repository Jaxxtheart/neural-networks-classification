Risk Management

The 7 Iron Rules

Break any of these and you invalidate the strategy

  • 🛑
    Never risk more than 2% of total portfolio per trade On a $1,441 account, max single trade loss = $28.82. Position size accordingly.
  • 🔒
    Hard stop-loss on every trade — no exceptions Set the stop when you enter. Never move it further from entry. Losses are information, not disasters.
  • ⚖️
    Minimum 2:1 reward-to-risk ratio required If you can't define a target that is 2× your stop distance, skip the trade entirely.
  • 📵
    No trading on major macro events without review Fed meetings, CPI prints, and ETF news cause extreme volatility. Stay flat or reduce size by 50%.
  • 🚫
    Zero leverage until Phase 2, maximum 2× in Phase 3 Leverage amplifies mistakes. Earn the right to use it by proving profitability first.
  • 📓
    Log every trade: entry, exit, reason, outcome Patterns in your winners and losers only emerge through documentation. Use a spreadsheet.
  • 🧘
    If down more than 10% in any week, stop trading for 3 days Emotional trading after a drawdown is the #1 account killer. Mandatory cooling-off period.
